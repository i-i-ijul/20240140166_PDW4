Who is Micah Bell?

One of The Best Representation of Betrayal

Micah Bell is one of the central antagonists in Red Dead Redemption 2 a member of the Van der Linde gang, Micah is known for his ruthless personality, manipulative behavior, and complete disregard for loyalty or morality. From the moment he joins the gang, Micah demonstrates himself as a violent and unpredictable outlaw. Unlike other members who still hold onto Dutch’s ideals of freedom and brotherhood, Micah operates purely on self-interest.
